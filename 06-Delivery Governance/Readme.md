
# Delivery Governance

## Overview

The Delivery Governance framework establishes the governance model for monitoring, controlling, and improving enterprise delivery performance across products, programs, and Agile teams.

It provides leadership with the visibility, governance controls, and decision-making mechanisms required to ensure predictable delivery, proactive risk management, and alignment with business objectives.

---

## Objectives

- Improve enterprise delivery governance and predictability.
- Enable data-driven delivery decisions.
- Monitor delivery performance across teams.
- Proactively manage delivery risks and dependencies.
- Drive continuous improvement and operational excellence.

## Expected Outcomes

- Improved delivery visibility and governance.
- Better delivery predictability and executive oversight.
- Faster identification and resolution of delivery risks.
- Stronger alignment between delivery execution and business objectives.
- Continuous improvement across delivery teams and programs.

---

## Key Insights

- Delivery governance enables informed decision-making through delivery transparency.
- Governance should focus on outcomes rather than activities.
- Early visibility of risks and dependencies improves delivery success.
- Performance measurement should drive continuous improvement.
- Effective governance supports Agile delivery without introducing unnecessary bureaucracy.

---

## Governance Principles

- Govern delivery through measurable outcomes.
- Enable proactive risk and dependency management.
- Maintain enterprise-wide delivery transparency.
- Support data-driven leadership decisions.
- Continuously inspect, learn, and improve delivery performance.
