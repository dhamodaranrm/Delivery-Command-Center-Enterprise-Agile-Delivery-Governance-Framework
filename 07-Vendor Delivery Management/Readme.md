
# Vendor Delivery Management

## Overview

The Vendor Delivery Management framework establishes the governance model for managing third-party vendors, implementation partners, and external delivery teams involved in enterprise Agile delivery.

It provides a structured approach to monitor vendor performance, manage delivery commitments, mitigate risks, and ensure alignment with business objectives and organizational governance standards.

---

## Objectives

- Establish consistent governance for vendor deliveries.
- Monitor vendor performance and delivery commitments.
- Manage vendor risks, dependencies, and escalations.
- Improve collaboration between internal and external delivery teams.
- Ensure vendors deliver value aligned with business expectations.

## Expected Outcomes

- Improved vendor accountability and delivery performance.
- Stronger governance of third-party engagements.
- Reduced delivery risks through proactive vendor management.
- Better alignment between vendor deliverables and business objectives.
- Effective collaboration and knowledge transfer across delivery teams.

---

## Key Insights

- Vendors should be governed as delivery partners, not just service providers.
- Clear SLAs, KPIs, and governance reviews improve delivery outcomes.
- Proactive communication and escalation reduce delivery risks.
- Performance measurement should focus on business value, quality, and predictability.
- Effective vendor governance strengthens enterprise delivery capability.

---

## Governance Principles

- Establish clear ownership and accountability.
- Monitor vendor performance through measurable KPIs.
- Manage risks and compliance proactively.
- Foster transparent communication and collaboration.
- Continuously improve vendor delivery through governance and feedback.
