
# Product Backlog Governance

## Overview

The Product Backlog Governance framework defines how delivery demand is captured, evaluated, prioritized, refined, and approved within the Delivery Command Center.

The objective is to ensure that backlog items are aligned to business priorities, delivery capacity, strategic objectives, and governance standards while maintaining transparency and delivery readiness.

---

## Objectives

- Establish a consistent backlog management process.
- Enable value-driven prioritization and decision-making.
- Improve backlog quality and delivery readiness.
- Maintain alignment between strategic objectives and delivery execution.
- Provide governance visibility across teams and stakeholders.

---

## Governance Principles

- Single source of truth for delivery demand.
- Value-based prioritization.
- Continuous backlog refinement.
- Clear ownership and accountability.
- Roadmap-driven delivery planning.
- Data-driven governance and reporting.
